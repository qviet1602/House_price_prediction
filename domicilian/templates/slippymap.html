Welcome to the GeoExplorer!

This is an interactive map that lets you explore neighborhoods to learn about:

  • Available Homes for Rent
  • Available Homes for Sale
  • Nearby Schools
  • Neighborhood Businesses

Naivgation

You can navigate the map by clicking and dragging to move.

You can zoom by either scrolling with your mouse wheel, pinching on your touch screen, or by using the +/- controls in the top left

Click and drag to move around the map in the currently zoomed area.

Exploring a Neighborhood

Click anywhere to drag a pin. In just a moment, you'll see different map markers

Hover over a map marker to see quick information about that marker

Click on the marker to see details. A details screen will open in this sidebar. (Note: You can navigate to the details screen at anytime by clicking the quad-cross in the sidebar

Filtering Data

Click on the Funnel in the sidebar to open the filtering menu.

Click on the icons to toggle whether or not you will see that marker category on the map

Built With

  • D3.js v5.1
  • Leaflet.js
  • Leaflet-Sidebar.js
  • Realtor.com API
  • Yelp Fusion API
  • Opencage Reverse Geocoding API

Details

Click on an icon on the map to find out more information about it!
